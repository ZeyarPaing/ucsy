Welcome

       The University of Computer Studies, Yangon (UCSY) located in the outskirts of Yangon (Hlaw Gar), is the leading IT and computer science university of Myanmar. The university, administered by the Ministry of Education, offers undergraduate and graduate degree programs in computer science and technology. The language of instruction at UCSY is English. Along with the University of Computer Studies, Mandalay, UCSY is one of two senior universities specialized in computer studies, and also one of the most selective universities in the country.

To produce more experts who have the practical ability to invent , install and apply computer hardware and software with a view to make Myanmar a modern, developed nation and to train Information Technology (IT) professionaols for Myanmar.

Multimedia aided active learning with hospitable and elegent teachers

Advanced complete Laboratories to build awesome projects

HISTORY

1971
It was originated as UCC in Yangon University (Hlaing Campus).
1973
It began offering a master's degree program (MSc. in Computer Science), and a graduate diploma program (Diploma in Automated Computing) .
1986
The center added B.C.Sc. (Bachelor of Computer Science) and B.C.Tech (Bachelor of Computer Technology) degree programs.
March 1988
It became independent as Institute of Computer Science and Technology (ICST).
July 1998
It was renamed as University of Computer Studies, Yangon (UCSY)
May 2001
It developed researching and establised PhD. degree programs.
